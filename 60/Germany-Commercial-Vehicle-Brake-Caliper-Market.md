Additionally, fixed calipers are durable and require less maintenance, although they are more expensive compared to floating calipers. Floating calipers are more cost-effective and simpler in design, making them a preferred choice for standard commercial vehicles such as delivery vans, smaller trucks, and transport vehicles.
