The two main types are membrane nitrogen generators and pressure swing adsorption (PSA) nitrogen generators. Membrane nitrogen generators use selective permeability to separate nitrogen from compressed air, offering a cost-effective and compact solution for generating nitrogen on-site. These systems are generally favored in smaller applications due to their low maintenance and energy-efficient design. Membrane generators are especially popular in automotive service centers and tire shops, where the volume of nitrogen required is moderate and the demand for efficiency is high. In the automotive sector, PSA generators are increasingly preferred by commercial tire shops and fleet service providers that require a continuous and high-purity nitrogen supply for larger-scale operations.
