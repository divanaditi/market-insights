Statutory pensions are typically the main source of retirement income for the majority of the population, although they often do not fully replace pre-retirement earnings. These plans are typically offered through employer-sponsored programs, individual insurance contracts, or investment products. Private pensions provide more flexibility compared to statutory plans, offering a wider range of investment options, including stocks, bonds, and real estate.
