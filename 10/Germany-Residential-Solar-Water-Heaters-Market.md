Passive systems are often used in smaller homes or in areas where water heating demands are moderate. There are two primary types of passive systems: thermosyphon and integral collector-storage systems. Thermosyphon systems use gravity and natural convection to move water, while integral collector-storage systems combine the collector and storage tank into one unit, making them compact and efficient.
