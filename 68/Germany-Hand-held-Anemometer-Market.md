This type of anemometer is widely used in fields such as HVAC (heating, ventilation, and air conditioning), environmental monitoring, and industrial applications where airflow measurement is critical for optimizing processes. The change in the electrical resistance of the wire is measured to determine wind speed. Hot-wire anemometers are particularly favored for their ability to measure low air velocities, which may not be detectable with other types of anemometers.
